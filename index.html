The lesson plan

Four weeks.
Real confidence.

A clear four-week series that builds practical knowledge one step at a time.

Every lesson starts with real questions.
We shape the conversation around the people in the room.

SafeSurf series4-week calendar
Week 1

Scam calls & messages

Spot urgent language, unexpected contacts, and requests for money or private information.

Week 2

AI voices

Learn why familiar voices can be faked and how to verify a surprising call before acting.

Week 3

AI images & videos

Practice noticing clues that a convincing photo or video may not tell the whole story.

Week 4

Safer accounts & next steps

Build simple password habits, protect personal details, and know who to ask for help.
